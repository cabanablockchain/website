Hardware - Firmware Engineer

At Cabana Blockchain, firmware is what gives our devices intelligence. We are looking for Firmware Engineers to join our team and help us create devices that go into people’s homes.

Responsibilities:

  • Work with our hardware team to design and program embedded systems
  • Work with our software team to bring our devices online

Requirements:

  • Bachelor’s degree (or equivalent) in electrical, computer, or firmware engineering
  • 5+ years of work experience
  • Experience programming embedded systems in C/C++
  • Programming drivers for microcontroller peripherals
  • Using real-time operating systems
  • Consuming APIs on embedded systems
